About us

CU-BIT is a consulting company from Croatia, specialized in IT services and business activities including project management, software development, software testing, development of IT documentation and providing technical support. The company was founded in April 2018 and operates in a network of its partners, both domestic and foreign markets. We build relationships with clients based on our expertise, modern business solutions and previous achieved results. We are focused on establishing long-term business relationship with included data protection and smart solutions in the best interest of our clients. The company currently counts 18 employees, number which is constantly increasing.
