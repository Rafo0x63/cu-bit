How to manage cookies

Our websites use the so-called cookies to ensure a better user experience and functionality. Cookies are small data files that are stored on your computer, mobile phone or other devices from which you access the website. Cookies make the interaction between you and our website faster and easier. We use cookies to distinguish you from other website users, provide you with a good experience when using the website, and provide you with personalised content.

You may refuse cookies, and in your web browsers, you may delete cookies and manage general cookie settings, but this may affect your ability to use the website. Rejection of cookies may result in some parts of the website not functioning normally or not being accessible. When using your device, you will come across third parties who use cookies. We are not responsible for these third parties or what they may place on your device or your browser.
